This site contains information about our great community located in West Cobb.

We are an active community of families, young couples, empty-nesters, and other lively people.

As neighbors and friends we like to gather and socialize throughout the year to celebrate seasonal events.

Our subdivision offers mature landscaping with 80 traditional style homes. In addition we have a Commons Area with the pavilion, swimming pool, tennis court, basketball and playground.

Our neighborhood is nestled close to Kennesaw Mountain National Park. Historical Marietta Square is located 5 miles from the subdivision that offers shopping and dining.

Battle Forest is great place to live for all ages and a place we call home.
